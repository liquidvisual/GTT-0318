Our Team

Dr Robert Wrixon

Starboard Global

Rob Wrixon is a Director of Starboard Global Ltd, a natural resource venture capital company based in Hong Kong. Starboard provides both project financing and project management services for mineral exploration and development projects. Rob has 17 years commercial experience in corporate strategy, mining M&A and exploration management. He was previously MD of two ASX listed mining companies: Haranga Resources (focusing on iron ore in Mongolia) and Manhattan Corporation (uranium in Western Australia). Rob spent five years in corporate strategy for Xstrata Coal in Sydney and Xstrata plc based in London, prior to the Glencore merger. He also served as Xstrata's representative on the board of the Cerrejon Coal joint venture in Colombia. He holds an honours degree in chemical engineering and a Ph.D in mineral engineering from the University of California, Berkeley.

Dr Lachlan Rutherford

Starboard Global

Lachlan Rutherford is Business Development Manager and County Manager – Spain of Starboard Global Ltd. Lachlan has 16 years’ experience in consultancy, mineral exploration, mining and research. He was Exploration Manager for ASX listed PepinNini Minerals Ltd for 3 years managing the company’s exploration activities in the Musgrave Province, central Australia. In 2011 Lachlan joined stockbroking firm Taylor Collison Ltd as a Resource and Mining Analyst where he was involved in project analysis and corporate strategy. Lachlan joined Starboard Global in August 2015. He holds a Honours degree in Geology, PhD in Geology and a MBA from the University of Adelaide.
